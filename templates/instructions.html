Instructions
You will be shown sets of images on the screen. Each image is a cropped screenshot from a video clip. The images represent their corresponding clips. All of the images start outside of the circle. You should move the images around inside the circle so that clips that are similar to each other in terms of the facial appearance of the main character are close. The more similar the two clips are in terms of facial appearance, the closer the images should be.
  • Hover the mouse above each image for the first time to show the corresponding clip.
  • Right-click an image to play the corresponding clip. Click a clip to close it.
  • Click an image to choose it. Click an empty space to move a chosen image there.
When you're finished, make sure all the images are inside the circle, then click 'Continue' to finalize your arrangement.
The experiment has 12 trials in total. The first trial is a practice trial with 3 clips, which are one dog and two cats, to help you get familiar with the experiment. Each of the other 3 trials approximately has 60 clips of faces and takes 10 minutes.
